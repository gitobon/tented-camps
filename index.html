Guests at Jambo Mutara Camp

Jambo Mutara Camp offers a range of facilities and amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for its guests. Some of these facilities include:

  1. Luxury Tents: The camp features 15 spacious and comfortable tents, each with its own private veranda and en-suite bathroom.
